Tiago Belintani

Research

Focus Areas

  • Triatominae: Systematics, taxonomy, morphology, and phylogenomics of blood-feeding insects.
  • Theraphosidae: Evolution, biogeography, and species delimitation in tarantulas.
  • Genomics & Bioinformatics: Comparative genomic analyses and evolutionary modeling.
  • Morphometrics: Geometric morphometrics and integrative taxonomy methods.

Current Projects

  • Phylogenomic reconstruction of Mepraia and related Triatominae species complexes.
  • Integrative taxonomy of South American tarantulas.
  • High-throughput DNA sequencing to investigate hybridization and evolutionary patterns.
  • Development of interactive datasets and phylogenetic pipelines for Neotropical arthropods.
