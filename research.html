Research

My research focuses on evolutionary biology, systematics, and phylogenomics of Neotropical organisms. Main areas include Triatominae (Chagas disease vectors) and Theraphosidae (tarantulas).

Research Themes

  • Evolutionary phylogenomics of insects and arachnids
  • Geometric morphometrics of anatomical structures
  • Species delimitation and integrative taxonomy
  • Comparative genomics and molecular systematics
  • Biogeography and diversification patterns
